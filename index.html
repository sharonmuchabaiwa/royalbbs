The Royal BBS is the funeral parlour that aim to ensure that your loved ones will receive the dignified buried, we are aiming to insure that all needs during those difficult time are been attended, to allow our members to morn their beloved family members and extended families.

Our Funeral parlour does not assess or accept members based on medical conditions, we are welcoming all members with all kind of disease or illness. Our aim is to insure that every Funeral is treated the same whether members are disadvantaged or not.

The Royal BBS is the Only Funeral parlour which assist its members nationwide (No place is far to us). We out shine our luxurious burials to our members at affordable premiums to insure that everyone qualifies to our packages including members which are depending on social grants. 

The Royal BBS is set to engage with the community societies to insure that they introduce this affordable luxurious burials to all their members. During the natural course of death our members will wait for 6 months period to claim but during accidental death once our members have paid their first premium, they are automatically covered for full service of the package they have chosen. 

The Royal BBS does not charge premiums based on ages, we are not funeral policy but we are burial cover that undertake or partake in actual burial to insure that all needs to the burial have been met.
