Adding and removing covered member after policy start date

a. You may add a new covered member at any time. If we accept a covered member in writing and the total premium is paid on the premium collection date, the new covered member’s cover will resume on the benefit start date.
b. If we have paid a benefit for a partner, you may add a new partner at any time.
 You must tell us you want to add a partner and pay the additional premium as once off payment.
 The new partner’s cover will start on his benefit start date, if we accept the partner in writing.
c. If you want to add a new born child, new born will be covered from date of birth if you give us a certified copy of the birth certificate of the new born child within 90 days from date of birth. If we do not receive this copy within 90 days from the birth of the new born, the new born child will be covered after the waiting period.
d. If you want to remove an insured person, the cover will end on the premium collection date following your request to us.

4. Waiting periods.

a. We will not pay benefits under the Standard cover, Gold cover, Superior cover, Royal cover, Executive cover and Presidential cover if the claim was caused by certain events during the waiting periods as set out below:
 The covered member died of natural causes.
 6 months before the waiting period complete.
 The insured person’s death was caused by suicide, attempted suicide or self-inflicted injury.
b. No waiting period will apply if an insured person dies because of an accident that happened during the period of insurance.

5. Limitations to what is to be paid if you don’t use our services for your burial.

a. Maximums under this policy
 The maximum policy amounts that we will pay for benefits under this policy are:
 R50 000 for the main insured person as a funeral benefit;
 R50 000 for the partner as funeral benefit;
 R20 000 for the parent or extended family member as a funeral benefit;
 R10 000 for a child younger than 6 years old;
b. Maximums under all The Royal BBS covers.
 If we cover an insured person under more than one The Royal BBS policy, and the insured person qualifies for more than one benefit, we will limit the total amount we will pay.
 The maximum total amount payable on all policies for Funeral benefits are:
 R85 000for an insured person who is 18 years or older;
 R30 000 for a child 14 years or older.
 R15 000 younger than 14 years
 R10 000 for a child younger than 6 years old.
